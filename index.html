Timeline

Critical Path to Launch

Jun 17-23 List setup window

List Foundation

Start with the 18k-contact list, then establish verified non-member segments.

  1. Contact Data Intellect to split the list into contacts who renewed within the past 10 years and contacts whose last payment was more than 10 years ago.
  2. Verify contact information through Bouncer and purge risky records.
Jun 24-30 Infrastructure window

Sender Infrastructure

Use the verified contact count to determine how many domains and inboxes are required.

  1. Purchase domains based on the post-purge list size.
  2. Purchase 2 inboxes per domain across Gmail, Zoho, and Microsoft.
  3. Implement DMARC, DKIM, and SPF for each domain.
Jul 1-3 Warmup launch

Warmup Starts

Connect inboxes to Smartlead/Instantly and confirm tracking, unsubscribe links, and sender settings.

  1. Place inboxes on a warmup sequence for the month of July.
Jul 6-10 Strategy framework

Segment Strategy

Define messaging angles for both non-member segments and outline the sequence structure.

  1. Develop strategy for contacts who renewed within the past 10 years.
  2. Develop strategy for contacts whose last payment was more than 10 years ago.
Jul 15-17 3-day hold

North Carolina Hotel Owners Conference

Reserve one travel day before the event, the event day itself, and one return travel day. Warmup continues automatically.

Jul 20-24 Copy build

Copy + Sequence Build

Draft the email touches, finalize the number of emails per sequence, and prepare A/B test variants.

  1. Finalize the number of emails for each sequence.
  2. Prepare subject lines, body copy, offers, and testing variations.
Jul 27-29 3-day hold

San Antonio Hotel Owners Conference

Reserve one travel day before the event, the event day itself, and one return travel day. Final testing work resumes afterward.

Jul 30-Aug 3 Final launch window

Final Prep + Launch

Use the final July prep window for QA, then launch on Monday, August 3.

  1. Send emails and start A/B testing for specific emails and touchpoints.
After send post-campaign

Review + Recycle

  1. Finish the campaign and re-evaluate the health of domains and inboxes.
  2. Recycle healthy domains and inboxes for another campaign.
